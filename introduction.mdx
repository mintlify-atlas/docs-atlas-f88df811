Sistema de Mantenimientos is a web application for managing the full lifecycle of equipment maintenance in multi-location organizations. It tracks physical assets across locations and rooms, assigns technicians to maintenance jobs, and keeps a complete history of all maintenance activity.

## Who it's for

The system is designed for:

- **IT administrators** who manage computer equipment across multiple sites and need to track maintenance schedules and histories.
- **Facility managers** responsible for ensuring equipment is serviced on time and faults are recorded.

  </Card>
  <Card title="Mantenimientos (Maintenance records)" icon="wrench">
    Logs of maintenance activity performed on equipment, including the problem description, type of maintenance, assigned technician, start date, end date, and resolution notes.
  </Card>
</Columns>

## Relationship hierarchy

Data in the system forms a clear parent-child hierarchy:

```
Sedes (Locations)
└── Salas (Rooms)
    └── Equipos (Equipment)
        └── Mantenimientos (Maintenance records)
```

This means:

- A **sede** contains one or more **salas**.
- A **sala** contains one or more **equipos**.
- An **equipo** accumulates **mantenimientos** over time.
- Each **mantenimiento** is carried out by one **monitor** (technician).
- Each **equipo** belongs to one **marca** (brand).

---
title: "Register new equipment"
description: "Step-by-step guide to adding a new piece of equipment to the inventory."
---

This workflow walks through registering a new equipment item from scratch, including setting up all required related records first.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Ensure a brand exists">
    Navigate to `/index.php` and confirm the manufacturer is listed. If not, add it:

    1. Enter the brand name in the **Nombre de Marca** field.
    2. Submit the form.

    Common manufacturers: Dell, Lenovo, Asus, Acer.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Ensure a location (sede) exists">
    Navigate to `/pages/sedes.php`. If the physical facility where the equipment will be located is not listed, add it:

    1. Enter the facility name in the **Nombre de Sede** field.
    2. Submit the form.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Ensure a room (sala) exists">
    Navigate to `/pages/salas.php`. If the room within that facility is not listed, add it:

    1. Enter the room name in the **Nombre de Sala** field.
    2. Select the parent location from the **Sede** dropdown.
    3. Submit the form.

    <Tip>
      Room names like `Sala de Cómputo 1` or `Laboratorio A` make it easier to identify equipment locations at a glance.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Navigate to the equipment page">
    Go to `/pages/equipos.php`. The form appears above the equipment table.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter the equipment code">
    Fill in the **Código** field with a unique identifier for this equipment item.

    <Tip>
      Use a consistent naming convention for equipment codes to make searching and auditing easier. A pattern like `[TYPE]-[YEAR]-[SEQUENCE]` works well (e.g., `PC-2024-001`, `LAP-2024-042`). Codes must be unique — the system will reject duplicates.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select the equipment type">
    Choose from the **Tipo** dropdown:

    - `Portatil` — for laptops and portable devices
    - `PC` — for desktop computers
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select the brand">
    Choose the manufacturer from the **Marca** dropdown. This list is populated from the brands registered in step 1.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set the entry date">
    Enter the **Fecha de Ingreso** — the date the equipment was put into service. This date is also used to calculate the first scheduled maintenance date (entry date + 180 days).
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select the room">
    Choose the physical room from the **Sala** dropdown. This list is populated from the rooms registered in step 3.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Submit the form">
    Click submit. The equipment is created with **Estado = Funcionamiento** (operational, `estado = 1`).
  </Step>

  <Step title="Verify the entry">
    Confirm the new equipment row appears in the table. Check that:

    - The **Estado** column shows `Funcionamiento`.
    - The **Fecha Siguiente Mantenimiento** column shows `fechaingreso + 180 days`.
    - The correct brand, room, and type are displayed.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What happens next

Once registered, the equipment is immediately available for maintenance scheduling. When the next maintenance date approaches, create a maintenance record at `/pages/mantenimientos.php`.
